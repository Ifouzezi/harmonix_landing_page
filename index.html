About the Project

Our project is a music streaming app called Harmonix. It uses machine learning to observe and generate music based on the user's listening habits. It offers a personalized music experience that is tailored to each user.

Essential Features

Personalized Music Experience

Harmonix learns about your listening habits and generates a personalized playlist of music that you're sure to love.

Discover New Music

Harmonix also suggests new music that you might like, based on your listening history.

Controllable Music Generation

You can also control the music generation process by providing feedback on the music that Harmonix generates. This helps Harmonix to learn your preferences and develop an even better theme for you.

Offline Listening

You can download music from Harmonix to listen to offline. This is great for when you're on the go or don't have an internet connection.
